Our beef

You know exactly what's in it, because we raised it.

“We're not willing to trade quality for speed or convenience.”

All of our beef is born on our farm and raised on 100% certified organic feed. While the grass is green, the cattle graze on pasture using rotational grazing and sustainable practices; in winter they're fed organic hay and corn. They always have fresh, clean water — and never any antibiotics, vaccines, hormones, or steroids.

Pasture-fed beef is naturally leaner — when you brown the ground beef, it isn't swimming in grease. Because the animals choose their own diet from a diverse organic pasture, they're healthier, and the meat is higher in heart-healthy Omega-3s. We deliver every animal to a family-owned butcher ourselves, so we know with certainty what's in your meat.

Pricing

Simple, by the pound.

Priced on hanging weight (the weight after butchering). Every price includes the processing fee, delivery to the butcher, and free delivery to your home. The final balance is set once the animal is processed, and is due at delivery.
